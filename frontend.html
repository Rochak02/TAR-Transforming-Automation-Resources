Transforming Automation Project (TAR) is an initiative to modernize and streamline processes using RPA, AI, and ML. It reduces manual work and increases efficiency across industries. Repetitive tasks are automated, freeing employees for higher-value roles. Accuracy and compliance improve with minimal human errors. Productivity rises as process cycles become faster and scalable. TAR supports digital transformation and data-driven decision-making. In healthcare, TAR automates patient data management and EHR updates. It speeds up medical billing and claims while reducing errors. AI aids in diagnostics and improves scheduling for patients. Overall, TAR reduces administrative burden and enhances patient care quality.
